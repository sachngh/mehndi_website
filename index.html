Welcome to Pavi Henna Art

Welcome to the innovative world of henna art & design, combining fusion designs from Arabic, Indian to Moghul. Pavi Henna Art offers a highly personalised and bespoke service to suit your individual needs and budgets.

Pavi Henna Art are a new generation of artists, using unique techniques together with natural ingredients for the mehndi, to add depth to its colour for your special occasions to last for days to come.

We at Pavi Henna Art, are available for all occasions such as weddings, mehndi parties, hen parties, fashion shows & corporate events to name a few.
